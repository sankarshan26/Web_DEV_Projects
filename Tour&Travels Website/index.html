North-Eastern Wonders

Day 1:Arrive in Guwahati, the gateway to North-East India, and transfer to your hotel Explore the city's bustling markets and visit the Kamakhya Temple, a prominent pilgrimage site

Day 2-3: Majestic Kaziranga National Park Embark on an exciting journey to Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site Enjoy thrilling jeep and elephant safaris to spot the iconic one-horned rhinoceros and other wildlife Immerse yourself in the picturesque landscapes and serene environment of the park

Day 4-5: Picturesque Shillong and Cherrapunjee Travel to the charming city of Shillong, also known as the "Scotland of the East" Explore the vibrant markets and visit the beautiful Umiam Lake Venture to Cherrapunjee, known as the wettest place on Earth, and marvel at its stunning waterfalls and lush greenery Visit the Living Root Bridges, a unique natural wonder

Day 6-7: Enchanting Gangtok and Tsomgo Lake Fly to Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, nestled amidst the Himalayan mountains Take in panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks and visit the famous Tsomgo Lake Explore the charming streets of Gangtok, visit monasteries, and indulge in local cuisine

Day 8-9: Serene Darjeeling and the Toy Train Journey to Darjeeling, famous for its tea plantations and panoramic views of the Kanchenjunga mountain range Experience a memorable ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, also known as the Toy Train Visit the Tiger Hill viewpoint to witness a breathtaking sunrise over the mountains

Royal Rajasthan Escapade

Day 1-2:Arrival in Delhi, explore the historic sites including Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and Qutub Minar.

Day 3-4:Travel to Jaipur, the Pink City, and visit the majestic Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and City Palace.

Day 5-6:Head to Jodhpur and explore the impressive Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the vibrant markets.

Day 7-8:Discover the desert city of Jaisalmer, visit the golden sand dunes, and explore the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort.

Day 9-10: Proceed to Udaipur, the City of Lakes, and enjoy the serene boat ride on Lake Pichola. Visit the grand City Palace and Saheliyon Ki Bari.

Day 11-12: Travel to Pushkar and experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Pushkar Camel Fair. Visit the famous Brahma Temple.

Day 13-14: Explore the city of Bikaner, known for its stunning palaces and forts, including Junagarh Fort and Lalgarh Palace.

Day 15: Return to Delhi for departure.

African Odyssey

Day 1-3:Arrive in Nairobi and explore the vibrant capital city. Visit the Nairobi National Park and learn about local wildlife conservation efforts. Enjoy cultural performances and savor authentic Kenyan cuisine.

Day 4-6:Travel to the iconic Maasai Mara Reserve. Witness the annual wildebeest migration, spot the Big Five on thrilling game drives, and interact with the Maasai people to learn about their traditions and way of life.

Day 7-9:Fly to Victoria Falls, one of the seven natural wonders of the world. Marvel at the awe-inspiring cascades, take a thrilling helicopter ride over the falls, and enjoy river cruises on the Zambezi River.

Day 10-12:Fly to Cape Town, known for its stunning natural beauty and vibrant culture. Explore Table Mountain, visit Robben Island, take a scenic drive along the Cape Peninsula, and indulge in wine tastings in the renowned Cape Winelands.

Day 13-15:Fly to Tanzania and embark on an unforgettable safari adventure in the Serengeti National Park. Witness the great wildebeest migration, spot lions, elephants, and giraffes, and enjoy immersive cultural experiences with local tribes.

Ultimate European Escape

Day 1: Arrival in Paris -Take a leisurely stroll along the Seine River and explore the charming neighborhoods and the iconic Eiffel Tower for breathtaking views of the city

Day 2-3: Exploring Paris -Enjoy a guided tour of the Louvre Museum, discover the romantic streets of Montmartre and visit the historic Sacré-Cœur Basilica. Indulge in delectable French cuisine at local bistros and experience Parisian café culture.

Day 4-5: Venice, Italy -Fly to the enchanting city of Venice, known for its picturesque canals and romantic ambiance, take a gondola ride along the Grand Canal and marvel at the stunning architecture and explore the hidden gems of Venice, including St. Mark's Square and the Doge's Palace

Day 6-7: Swiss Alps Adventure - Travel to Switzerland and immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the Swiss Alps, enjoy thrilling outdoor activities like hiking, skiing, or snowboarding and relax in a cozy mountain chalet and savor trditional Swiss delicacies.

Day 8-9: London, United Kingdom - Fly to vibrant London and experience the perfect blend of history and modernity and visit iconic landmarks such as the Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and the Tower of London and explore the vibrant neighborhoods of Camden Market and Covent Garden.

Enchanting India

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi -Arrive in the bustling city of Delhi and transfer to a comfortable hotel, explore the vibrant streets of Old Delhi, visiting historic landmarks like Jama Masjid and Red Fort and experience the local flavors with a street food tour, savoring delicious chaat and kebabs.

Day 2-3: Agra and the Taj Mahal -Journey to Agra and witness the mesmerizing Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, Explore the grand Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its fascinating history and visit the serene Fatehpur Sikri, an abandoned Mughal city known for its architectural brilliance.

Day 4-5: Jaipur, the Pink City -Travel to Jaipur and immerse yourself in the rich culture and heritage of Rajasthan and discover the majestic Amber Fort, where you can enjoy an elephant ride to the fort entrance and explore the City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar, showcasing Jaipur's architectural marvels

Day 6-7: Udaipur, the City of Lakes - Fly to the romantic city of Udaipur, famous for its stunning lakes and palaces, Cruise along Lake Pichola and marvel at the picturesque scenery and the iconic Lake Palace and visit the magnificent City Palace and stroll through the colorful markets of Udaipur

Day 8-9: Mumbai and the Gateway of India - Fly to the vibrant city of Mumbai, the entertainment and financial capital of India, explore the bustling streets of Colaba and visit the iconic Gateway of India and experience the bustling markets of Crawford Market and indulge in mouthwatering street food
